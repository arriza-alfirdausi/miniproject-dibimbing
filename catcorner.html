American Shorthair

This gorgeous and athletic cat was shaped and designed to be the ultimate in pest control. With a broad chest, well-muscled body, strong jaws, and thick neck, the American shorthair is the quintessential example of feline beauty.

|credit: Catherine Falls Commercial / getty

Bengal

Bengal cats are a lithe and agile breed and generally weigh between 3.6 and 6.8 kilograms. Their lengthy legs make them excellent jumpers. Bengal cats are generally have a larger and longer body compared to domestic cats.

|credit: Seregraff / getty

Exotic Shorthair

Exotic shorthair cats are a medium-sized breed with beautiful round heads, eyes, and bodies. Most will tip the scales between 4.5 and 5.4 kilograms. They are a quiet and curious breed that are a bit more active than their long-haired cousins

|credit: Joke50E / shutterstock

Persian

Persian cats are a medium-sized breed, with female Persians weighing between 3.1 and 4.5 kilograms and males at a slightly larger 4 to 5.9 kilograms. These iconic felines are well-known for their thick fur coats, large eyes, and cute smooshy faces.

|credit: Rafael Elias / getty

Siamese

Siamese cats have a lean, lengthy frame with slim but muscular bodies. Because of their genetics, these felines will always have piercing blue eyes. Their long, thin limbs and tails, coupled with high-contrast colorpoint pattern, makes them easily recognizable.

|credit: Laura Drake / getty

Burmese

There are two types Burmese cats. The European Burmese (sometimes called the "traditional variant") is the more slender of the two. While the American Burmese (some called them the "contemporary variant") is notably stockier.

|credit: Julia Janevica / getty

Munchkin

These petite cats are noticeably low to the ground, with legs that are about 7.6 centimeters shorter than the average feline. The rest of the Munchkin body is pretty typical of your run-of-the-mill house cat, with most of adults weighing somewhere between 2.7 – 4 kilograms.

|credit: Michael Beder / getty

RagaMuffin

Everything about the RagaMuffin gives off a feeling of substance. With a squarish, muscled body and medium-long to long hair that tends to be voluminous. Female RagaMuffins weigh about 5.4 kilograms while male cats weigh 9 kilograms.

|credit: Eveymmnt / adobe stock

Scottish Fold

Scottish folds are a medium-sized, stocky breed that loves human attention. These gorgeous cats have rounded heads, short necks, and large eyes that, combined with their folded-over ears, give them a unique, owl-like look.

|credit: Nico de Pasquale Photography / getty

Somali

Somalis are medium large cats with the average weight of around 2.7 to 4.5 kilograms. With large almond eyes, large pointed ears, and bold facial markings that are another hallmark of the breed. They comes in four recognized colors of red, ruddy, fawn, and blue.

|credit: Errol the Cat / twitter
